Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ExamSys and get the support you need to succeed.

How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address. A reset link will be valid for 24 hours.
What are the system requirements?
You need a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and a stable internet connection. We recommend at least 4GB RAM and a dual-core processor for optimal performance.
How can I contact support?
Reach us at support@examsys.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST). We typically respond within 1 business day.
Are exams proctored?
Yes, some exams use online proctoring with webcam and screen monitoring. Check your exam details in the dashboard for specific requirements and guidelines.
